For 辛 Metal under Fire, emotional suppression is a primary health risk. The absent Water element means unfelt grief, unprocessed sadness, and suppressed emotions tend to accumulate in the Metal organs — the lungs and skin. Chinese medicine teaches that grief is held in the lungs; when 辛 Metal cannot release, the body speaks. Investing in emotional release — through therapy, creative practice, movement, or trusted intimate relationships — is not a luxury for you. It is preventive medicine.
